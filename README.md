# Simple Scientific Calculator

This is a clean and easy **Scientific Calculator** made for school students. It helps you solve math problems fast on your phone or computer.

### Why this is good?
* **Simple Style:** It is only Black and White. No confusing colors.
* **Works on Mobile:** It looks great on every phone screen.
* **One Line Title:** The heading stays in one line and does not break.
* **Full Screen:** Click the start button to use it in full screen like a real app.
* **Privacy First:** We do not save your data. It is 100% safe.

### Features
* **Basic Math:** Plus, Minus, Divide, Multiply.
* **Advanced Math:** Sin, Cos, Tan, Log, and Square Root.
* **Legal Pages:** Includes Privacy Policy and Terms for Google AdSense.

### How to use?
1. Open the website.
2. Read the simple math notes on the main page.
3. Click the **"Start Tool"** button.
4. Solve your math problems.
5. Click **"Exit Tool"** to go back.
